Hit me hard and soft

He is the fourth album of the singer with the collaboration of her brother Finneas O'Connel. The album's title came from a conversation with Finneas, when Billie mistakenly thought that the name of a synthesizer in Logic pro was "Hit me hard and soft. In an anrticle in Rolling Stone, she explained the choice of the title : " I thought it was a perfect encapsulation of what this album is about. It's an impossible request : You can't be hit hard and soft. I'm pretty extreme person, and i really like it when things are very intense physically, but I also like it when things are very tender and soft. I want two things at once. So I thought it was a really good way to describe myself."

Happier than ever

Happier than ever is the third album by Billie. He was released on July 30th 2021. Billie co-wrote the album with her brother Finneas O'Connell, who also produced the album and played every instrument. She cited self-reflection during the COVID-19 pandemic as the biggest inspiration for the record.

When we all fall asleep, where do we go ?

He is the second album, it was released on March 29,2019. She was 17 at the time of the release, largely wrote album with ther brother Finneas who produced it at his small bedroom. Musically he is a pop, electropop, avant-pop and art-pop record, though it also features influences from hip hop and industrial music. The songs explore themes such as modern youth, drug addiction, heartbreak, suicide and mental health, with lyrical sensibilities of humor and horror. The album's title comes from a line in the song "Bury a friend". Billie said the album was inspired in part by lucid dreaming and night terrors, which are reflected on the cover photo.
